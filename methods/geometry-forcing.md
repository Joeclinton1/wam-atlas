 1003: we apply bilinear interpolation to reduce the spatial dimensions from the original resolution to a
 1004: manageable 512×512 size.
 1005: The alignment is performed using a Conv3D-based projector that operates on the latent dimensions.
 1006: To accommodate multi-layer and multi-target alignment scenarios, we initialize independent pro-
 1007: jectors for each feature layer and target representation. This design ensures effective dimensional
 1008: compatibility between the U-ViT feature space and the target geometric representations while main-
 1009: taining computational efficiency.

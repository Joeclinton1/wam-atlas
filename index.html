    What Is A WAM?

    A video action model is a robot policy or policy-learning pipeline where video prediction, video representation learning, or video-derived latent dynamics is central to action generation. The useful question is not whether a model can make a plausible movie. The useful question is which future variable survives into the deployed controller.

    Uncertainty Notes

    The cards normalize paper claims into a common architecture schema. A low uncertainty note means the local extracted paper text or survey table directly supports the card. A medium or high note means the card is synthesized from survey notes, source filenames, and BibTeX metadata and should be verified against the paper before citation.
